    Refund Policy

    At Grtak, we take the privacy of our customers very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information that you provide to us through our website.

    We at Grtak value our customers and want to ensure that you are completely satisfied with your order. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we have a refund policy in place to address any issues you may have.

    We collect the following types of personal information from our customers:

    • You are eligible for a refund if you received an incorrect order or if there was a mistake in your order that was our fault.
    • You are eligible for a refund if you received a damaged or spoiled pizza due to our mistake or error.
    • You are not eligible for a refund if you simply do not like the taste of the pizza.

    Refund Process:

    • If you are eligible for a refund, please contact our customer service team within 24 hours of receiving your order. You can reach us by phone, email, or through our website contact form.
    • Please provide us with your order number, the reason for the refund request, and any relevant photos or information that can help us address the issue.
    • Our team will review your request and may ask for additional information or clarification if needed.
    • If your request is approved, we will issue a refund to the original payment method used for the order.

    Refund Timeline:

    • Once your refund request is approved, we will process the refund within 5-7 business days.
    • Please note that the time it takes for the refund to reflect on your account may vary depending on your bank or credit card provider.

    Cancellations:

    • If you need to cancel your order, please contact us as soon as possible.
    • If your order has not been prepared or dispatched, we will cancel the order and issue a full refund.
    • If your order has already been prepared or dispatched, we may not be able to cancel the order and issue a refund. In such cases, please contact us to discuss the situation.

    We strive to provide the best possible service to our customers and will do everything we can to address any issues you may have. Thank you for choosing Grtak and we look forward to serving you soon!
